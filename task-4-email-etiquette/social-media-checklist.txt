Do's: 

Use a professional-looking profile picture on sites like LinkedIn.

Fact-check information before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.

Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts.

Engage in positive discussions and support your peers' achievements.

Think twice before posting—if you wouldn't say it in person, don't post it.


Don'ts: 

Don't post or share offensive, hateful, or discriminatory content.

Don't share sensitive personal info like your home address or OTPs.

Don't engage with "trolls" or get into heated arguments with strangers.

Don't use unprofessional language or slang when interacting with faculty/mentors.

Don't tag people in embarrassing photos without their permission.
