Prevention Checklist: 



Check the sender's full email address for tiny spelling errors. 



Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank. 



Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all academic and financial accounts. 



Avoid clicking links in "urgent" or "threatening" emails; log in directly through the official website instead. 



Verify any unexpected payment requests by calling the person or department directly. 



Keep your browser and antivirus software updated to catch malicious sites. 



Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
