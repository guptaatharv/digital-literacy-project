Scenario: The "Urgent Scholarship" Phishing Attack

In this scenario, a student receives an email that looks exactly like an official university communication. The subject line reads "Action Required: Scholarship Refund Pending." The email claims the student is eligible for a 5,000 INR refund but must "verify" their bank details within 2 hours or the link will expire.



The student clicks a link leading to a fake login page that looks like the college portal. Once they enter their credentials and UPI PIN, the attacker gains full access. This typically happens through "spoofing," where the sender's address is masked to look official. The consequences are immediate financial loss and potential identity theft. Students are usually targeted because they frequently handle online payments and may react quickly to "urgent" academic alerts.
